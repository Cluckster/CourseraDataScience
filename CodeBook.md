## variables used
subject - numeric identifier, from 1 - 30 that uniquely identifies the subject that performed the activity

label - identifies the activity name as:

        * WALKING
        * WALKING_UPSTAIRS
        * WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
        * SITTING
        * STANDING
        * LAYING
